Column names were changed from
Acc to Accelerometer
Gyro to Gyroscope
BodyBody to Body
Mag to Magnitude
^t to Time
^f to Frequency
tbody to Time Body
std to Standard Deviation
-freq to Frequency
code column was renamed to "acitivities"
